Virat Kohli

Biography

Virat Kohli borns in 5 November 1988. He is an Indian international cricketer & the former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is a right-handed batsman.He currently represents RCB in the IPL(Indian Premire League).He holds the record as the highest run-scorer in T20I & IPL.He ranks third in ODI, & stands as the fourth-highest in international cricket.In 2013, Kohli was ranked number One batsman in the ICC rankings for ODI batsmen in year 2013.
