Virat Kohli

Biography

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is a right-handed batsman who currently represents RCB in the IPL (Indian Premier League). He holds the record as the highest run-scorer in T20I and IPL. He ranks third in ODI and stands as the fourth-highest in international cricket. In 2013, Kohli was ranked the number One batsman in the ICC rankings for ODI batsmen.
