Virat Kohli

Biography

Virat Kohli was born on 5th November 1988. He is an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is a right-handed batsman and currently represents RCB in the IPL (Indian Premier League). Kohli holds records as the highest run-scorer in T20I and IPL, and ranks third in ODIs. He was ranked number One in the ICC rankings for ODI batsmen in 2013.
